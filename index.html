about

Hi, I'm Ann

I'm a student at Columbia University studying Computer Science and Mathematics!

Growing up, I was fascinated by the ways I could use creativity to make a positive impact. From painting murals at senior apartments to sketching 1-minute caricatures for fundraising, I learned how small acts of design and art could create meaningful connections and outcomes.

Now, with my technical skills, I'm channeling that same creativity into building solutions that merge design with engineering. I'm especially interested in developing websites and tools that are intuitive, engaging, and accessible to all. I aim to reflect both my artistic roots and my commitment to creating technology that makes a difference.
